\hrefsection{comp.pp-fcsrng}{\texorpdfstring{Definition of the extended family
  \secitemfont{\nounderscore{FCS_RNG}}}{Definition of the extended family FCS\_RNG}}

\minisec{Family Behaviour}

This family defines requirements to the creation of random numbers that are used
for cryptographic applications.

\minisec{Component Leveling}

\vspace{2ex}
\framebox{\secitem{FCS_RNG} \hspace{2em} Random number generation}%
\rule[.5ex]{2cm}{0.1pt}%
\framebox{1}
\par
\vspace{2ex}

\secitem{FCS_RNG.1} ``random number generation'' requires the identification of
the type of the random number generator and a list of its security capabilities. A
quality metric for the generated random numbers must be specified. The
evaluation and processing of the random numbers has to rely upon this quality
metric.


\minisec{Management: \secitem{FCS_RNG.1}}

There are not management activities foreseen for this component.

\minisec{Audit: \secitem{FCS_RNG.1}}

There are no events identified that must be audited, if \formatsfr{FAU\_GEN} is part of the PP/ST.

\minisec{{\normalsize\secitem{FCS_RNG.1}}\nopagebreak\\{\small Random number generation}}
\vspace{1ex}

\hierarchicalto{No other component}
\dependencies{No dependencies}

\begin{sfrdefinitionlist}
\item[{\normalfont{\small\secitem{FCS_RNG.1.1}}}] The TSF shall provide a {[}selection:
  physical, non-physical true, deterministic, hybrid physical, hybrid
  deterministic] random number generator that implements:
  {[}assignment: list of security capabilities{]}.
\end{sfrdefinitionlist}

\begin{sfrdefinitionlist}
\item[{\normalfont{\small\secitem{FCS_RNG.1.2}}}] The TSF shall provide random numbers that
  meet {[}assignment: a defined quality metric{]}.
\end{sfrdefinitionlist}

\minisec{Rationale for the introduction of the extended family}

According to the definiton of \objlink{oe.realtimeclock} in \citepp{} the TOE is
responsible for the generation of random numbers.
